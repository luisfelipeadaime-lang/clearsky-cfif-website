Who Can Contribute

CFIF is designed for primary fuel suppliers registered under Canada's Clean Fuel Regulations. If your organization produces or imports liquid fossil fuels and is subject to CFR compliance obligations, you may be eligible to contribute to CFIF.

Contributions are structured to align with your regulatory obligations and provide full compliance credit. Our team works directly with each contributor to ensure the process is straightforward, transparent, and tailored to your organization's compliance needs.
